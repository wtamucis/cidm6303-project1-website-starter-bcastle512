All listed prices are starting prices and may vary depending on the specific details of your project. Final pricing may increase based on factors such as fabric selection, complexity of design, number of cording colors requested, and any additional custom work such as embroidery on the back of the piece. Each project is unique, and pricing reflects the materials and time required to achieve a high-quality, professional finish.

Heavier or specialty fabrics, upgraded materials, and custom requests may result in additional costs. Likewise, projects that include multi-color cording, specialty trims, or personalized embroidery on the back will be quoted accordingly once the full design details are reviewed. I am always happy to discuss options in advance so you can choose finishes that best fit both your vision and budget.

A discount will be applied when customers provide their own finishing supplies, including fabric and cording threads, along with their needlepoint piece. This helps offset material costs and will be reflected in the final invoice once all materials are received and evaluated.
