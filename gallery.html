Needlepoint Finishing Image Gallery

Round Ornaments

Your stitched piece is transformed into a beautifully finished round ornament, carefully constructed to enhance its shape and design. Each piece is assembled with attention to detail, ensuring a smooth, balanced finish that highlights your stitching while creating a timeless piece perfect for seasonal decorating or year-round display.

Square/Rectangle Finishing

Square and rectangular designs are finished with clean lines and structured edges to complement the geometry of your stitching. This style provides a crisp, tailored look that showcases both traditional and contemporary designs. Pieces can be finished with a loop for hanging or as a hanging sign, offering flexibility in how your ornament is displayed. The result is a polished, versatile piece suited for a variety of decorative settings.

Odd-Shaped Ornaments

Uniquely shaped designs are carefully finished to follow the contours of the original piece, creating a distinctive and custom result. Each ornament is thoughtfully constructed to preserve the integrity of the design while enhancing its visual appeal. Please note that in pieces with very tight corners, intricate points, or delicate edges, some fine details may be softened or slightly lost during the finishing process in order to ensure durability and a clean, secure final result.

Stand Up Finishing

This option turns your needlepoint into a freestanding display piece, finished with a weighted or structured base that allows it to stand securely on a shelf, mantel, or table. It’s an excellent way to showcase decorative motifs and detailed designs in a bold, visible format.

Tray Insert Finishing

Your needlepoint is professionally finished to fit neatly into a serving tray, creating a functional and decorative accent piece. This option allows your artwork to be easily displayed and swapped out seasonally while adding a personalized touch to entertaining and home décor.

Mini Stocking Finishing

Your stitched design is finished into a small, decorative stocking ideal for holiday displays, gift card holders, or festive décor. Each stocking is carefully shaped and lined (if applicable) to ensure a polished look while preserving the charm of your original stitching.
