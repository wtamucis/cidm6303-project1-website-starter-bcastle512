Thank you for visiting my finishing website. I truly appreciate your trust in allowing me to complete your needlepoint and transform your stitched work into beautifully finished pieces ready for display. Each project is handled with care and attention to detail, with the goal of honoring the time, effort, and creativity you have put into your needlework. Whether your piece becomes an ornament, a stand up, a mini stocking or a tray insert, my aim is to bring out its full charm while preserving its craftsmanship for years to come. I look forward to working with you and helping your needlepoint find its perfect final presentation.

Finishing Options:

I offer a variety of finishing options designed to showcase your needlepoint in a way that best suits both the design and its intended use. Each finish is carefully constructed to complement your stitching while creating a polished, professional presentation that can be enjoyed for years to come. Below are the finishing options currently available:

  • Ornament [round, square or odd-shaped] Finishing
  • Stand Up Finishing
  • Tray Insert Finishing
  • Mini Stocking Finishing

Turnaround time for all finishing projects is approximately 3–4 months from the time your piece is received, as each item is completed with careful attention to detail and quality craftsmanship. A discount is offered if you provide your own fabric and cording threads, which will be applied accordingly once your materials are received and reviewed. Please ensure all pieces are clean, properly labeled, and securely packaged when sent in. If you have any special requests, custom ideas, or questions about your finishing options, feel free to reach out prior to sending your project. I am always happy to help guide you through the process and ensure your finished piece turns out exactly as you envision.
