This finishing form is designed to collect detailed instructions for transforming a completed needlepoint canvas into a finished object. Needlepoint finishing is the process of turning stitched work into functional or decorative items such as pillows, ornaments, or tray inserts. Because each piece is unique, clear communication between the stitcher and finisher is essential to ensure the final product reflects the intended design and materials.

To complete this form, provide your contact information and select the desired finishing style. Indicate all relevant details, including size, orientation, trim, backing fabric, and any special requests. If needed, sketches or additional notes may be included to clarify placement or design choices. The more specific your instructions, the more accurately the finisher can execute your vision.

Once completed, this form should be submitted along with the stitched canvas and any additional materials required for finishing. Be sure all fields are filled out to avoid delays or misinterpretation. Finishing is a detailed and skilled process, and complete, accurate information helps ensure a high-quality final result that aligns with your expectations.
